Avoided Costs Model

Calculate ROI for your facility in 60 seconds.

Enter sludge volume and facility parameters — the simulator will instantly show CapEx, annual savings, land value, and payback period after funding.

How we calculate → methodology

Traditional payback models for new technologies rely on potential revenue from product sales — hydrochar, energy, fertilizers. We deliberately do NOT use this approach.

We do not count revenue from hydrochar sales — we count how much you STOP PAYING. The costs your facility currently spends on sludge disposal (transport, polymers, landfill, reclamation) — is the avoided cost after BTC installation.

Now your facility PAYS annually

  • Dewatering polymers: €40–80/t
  • Transport to disposal site: €20–50/t
  • Landfill or incinerator Gate Fee: €60–160/t
  • Eco-fee / landfill fines: €40–120/t
  • Sludge fields reclamation: €20–50/t

→ and these amounts grow annually due to EU ETS and UWWTD

After BTC — one-time investment replaces endless payments

All these items disappear from the operating budget. After payback, 100% of former costs become net profit — forever, every year the plant operates.

Why is this model more reliable than a revenue forecast?

Current disposal costs are auditable items in your budget. They do not need to be forecast — they already exist. The hydrochar market is in its infancy and depends on economic conditions. The avoided costs model does not depend on sale price, demand, or regulatory changes. Therefore, it passes more easily through city councils, grant committees, and bank scoring for EBRD / EIB loans.

Additionally — but NOT in the base calculation

Potential revenues from hydrochar sales (coal substitute, soil amendments) and carbon credits exist but the market is volatile. The simulator shows the worst-case scenario — so any real number will be a pleasant surprise.

CapEx

~€250 per 1 t/year capacity. Modular design — price scales linearly. After a 75% EU grant, the real municipal share is ~€62/t.

Grants

EU LIFE / Horizon Europe / KRPOiŚ / UA Recovery Fund — up to 60–85% CapEx. The model defaults to 75% — a conservative estimate.

ROI Formula

(CapEx after grant − Land Asset) ÷ Annual savings

Land Asset

1.5 m² per 1 t/year capacity (sludge beds + sanitary zone). Upon closing sludge fields, the land returns to economic use and is included in the project balance.

Base rates and sources

Disposal costs — industry averages from Polish tenders 2022–2024. Biogas tariff — EU ETS mid-price. Sludge norm — JRC FDM BREF 2019. All values editable in the calculator.
