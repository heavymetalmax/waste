How it Works

4 Stages of Molecular Rearrangement

Fast physical-thermal BioTC process replicating the centuries-long natural coal formation cycle in just 2 hours.

01

Preheating

Dewatered organic feedstock (approx. 80% moisture) is fed into the system and preheated using thermal recovery from discharge lines, ensuring minimal external energy consumption.

02

Molecular Compounding Reaction

The mixture is heated to 180-220°C under autogenous pressure of 15-25 bar. Water remains liquid while the cell structure of microorganisms is disrupted, releasing bound water.

03

Mechanical Dewatering

The carbonized coal slurry releases water easily. It is fed to a filter press where moisture is reduced from 80% to 35-40% without expensive coagulants.

04

Drying & Granulation

Dewatered Eco-Coal is dried using low-grade heat to a residual moisture content of <10%. The final product is clean Eco-Coal, ready for combustion or sale.
