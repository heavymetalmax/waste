The Clean Water Paradox

We cleaned our rivers, but we poisoned our soil.

Every wastewater treatment plant does half the job perfectly: it returns clean water to our rivers. But where does the dirt go?

The Toxic Accumulation Liability

Traditional treatment generates pathogen-ridden sewage sludge. Dumping mud in landfills or mixing it with lime simply hides the smell. It does not destroy microplastics, pharmaceuticals, and PFAS ("forever chemicals"). They wait in the soil, ready to seep back into our drinking water.

As EU environmental requirements tighten, water companies face skyrocketing gate fees, stricter transport regulations, and chemical bans. Landfills are filling up. The "cleaning water" loop is broken because we are leaving a toxic trail on land.

Zero Sludge. Net-Positive Energy.

We don’t bury the problem. We rearrange it.

Using BioTC’s advanced Organic Composition Unit, we take wet organic waste and put it through a fast, pressurized thermal loop. In just two hours, the molecular structure of the sludge is permanently transformed.

Our patented HTC process does not burn the waste—it pressure-cooks it, mimicking natural coal creation in minutes. The result is a complete eradication of sludge volume, turning your biggest liability into two valuable assets:
