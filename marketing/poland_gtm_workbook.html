1ICP & Target Market Segmentation

In Poland, sewage sludge treatment is driven by strict EU directives. We need to decide which segment to prioritize for our initial outbound campaigns.

Municipal (B2G): MPWiK

• Heavy regulatory pressure (Directive 2024/3019)
• Large volumes, eligible for NFOŚiGW grants
• Subject to Public Procurement Law (long sales cycle)

Industrial (B2B): Factories

• Food processing, chemical, paper plants
• Short sales cycle, direct executive negotiations
• Urgent ESG/carbon footprint requirements
