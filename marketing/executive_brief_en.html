BioTC: Turn Sludge Liability into Clean Energy

1. Executive Summary

BioTC (developed by BioTheCon and represented by BTC Consulting Sp. z o.o.) is an integrated clean-tech solution that solves the final, most critical bottleneck of wastewater treatment: sewage sludge and organic waste accumulation.

By combining Thermal Hydrolysis (TH) and Hydrothermal Carbonization (HTC) in a single, closed-loop system, BioTC converts wet, hazardous organic sludge into sterile, high-energy Eco-Coal and clean, pathogen-free water on-site.

Waste Reduction

Sludge volume is compressed by up to 80% (5x volume reduction), removing the water component and drastically decreasing logistical costs.

Energy Production

Converts wet feedstock into dry Eco-Coal biofuel (20–25 MJ/kg), usable in industrial boilers for heating or sold on the market.

2. The Clean-Water Paradox & Regulatory Pressure

Wastewater treatment plants (WWTPs) excel at returning clean water to rivers. However, this process filters out a toxic, wet, pathogen-ridden residue: sewage sludge. Current management methods are facing critical challenges:

The Logistic & Financial Strain

Sewage sludge consists of 80% moisture. Traditional hauling and landfill disposal are expensive because operators are essentially paying to transport water. Environmental taxes and landfill dumping fees are rising exponentially, making old disposal methods unsustainable.

Tighter EU Directives

Under new EU regulations (including **Directive 2024/3019**), agricultural spreading and landfilling of raw sludge are being banned. Microplastics, pharmaceutical traces, and PFAS ("forever chemicals") must be completely destroyed to prevent soil and groundwater contamination.

3. The Technology: Integrated TH + HTC

Instead of relying on energy-intensive air-drying or incineration, BioTC uses a pressurized thermochemical process that mimics natural coal formation over millions of years, compressing it into a 2-hour cycle.

1. Wet Feedstock 80% moisture sludge from dewatering press
→
2. Reactor (HTC) Cooked at 200°C under 20 bar pressure
→
3. Press Chamber Mechanical pressing down to 30% moisture
→
4. Outputs Solid Eco-Coal + Sterile Recycled Water
  • Thermal Destruction: High-pressure cooking at 180–220 °C breaks down cellular walls and completely destroys 100% of pathogen structures, bacteria, microplastics, hormone residues, and PFAS.
  • Physical Phase Change: The sludge loses its colloidal structure. Water, which was chemically bound inside sludge cells, is released and can be easily pressed out mechanically without high heat.
  • Digestion Booster (HTC-D): The liquid run-off after mechanical press is rich in soluble organics. Returning it back to anaerobic digestion tanks stimulates additional **biogas yields by up to 60%**.

4. Value-Added Outputs

The BioTC process converts a toxic municipal liability into two valuable commodities:

Eco-Coal (Hydrochar)

A dry, sterile, and odorless solid carbon fuel with high heating density:

  • Energy Density: 20–25 MJ/kg (equivalent to coal).
  • CO2 Neutrality: Classified as sustainable biofuel.
  • Uses: Combustion in boilers, cement plant feedstock, soil amendment.

Clean Recycled Water

All contaminants are neutralized by the high-pressure steam:

  • Safety: Free from PFAS, microplastics, and active bacteria.
  • Recycling: Safely returned to the WWTP inlet.
  • Volume: Fully sterilised water run-off.
